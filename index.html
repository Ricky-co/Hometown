Visit Knoxville

Enjoy nature and sports in this beautiful town nestled in East Tennessee

Top things to check out in Knoxville

The Knoxville Zoo

Emark on a global safari without leaving the city! From cute otters, to beautiful tigers, this Zoo has a lot to offer.

World's Fair Park

This Park is home to the iconic symbol, the Sunsphere. Constructed for the exposition back in 1982. Explore the blend of nature and culture in this vibrant urban oasis.

Neyland Stadium

Home of the UT Vols, Neyland Stadium offers an experience like never before. If you're a football fan, you'll love this thrilling spectacle of College Football.
